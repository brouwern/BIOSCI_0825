BIOSCI 0835 Our Changing World Syllabus

Topic 37 Tests

37.1 General Test policies & Information:

  1. Tests will be administered in-class but taken on Canvas.
  2. There will be 4 mini-tests during the semester. 1 will be dropped as detailed elsewhere in the syllabus.
  3. There are approximately 4.5 weeks of material per test.
  4. I will not answer questions during the test. This is my normal policy during my other classes, and also is in line with COVID social distancing policies.
  5. Though I do not currently plan on doing this, I reserve the right to require anyone with an accommodation (e.g. DRS, quarantine) to take the test remotely to log into Zoom and have your camera on while taking tests.

37.2 Testing technology policies

  1. If you accidently exit and re-enter the exam you must restart it.
  2. It is your responsibility to assure that you have adequate power to your device and internet access.
  3. Devices can be borrowed from the library if necessary.
  4. If a power or WiFi issue beyond our control impacts the class the test may be re-schedule.
